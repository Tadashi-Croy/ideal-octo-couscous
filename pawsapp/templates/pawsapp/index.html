Services

All consults will be done over Zoom. Lesson locations will be determined during the video call. Discounts available for rescue dogs, foster dogs, and shelter dogs.
Walk and Train

I come to your home and take your dog for a daytime 30 - 60 minute walk providing exercise and training. This is a great service for those with energetic or unruly dogs! I leave you daily reports with your dog’s progress and the techniques that I am using so that you can implement them as well. Prices start at $25/walk.

Day Training

Standard one hour classes to help your dog be the best they can be.

Puppy Training

One on one training sessions for puppies. Length of session depends on needs but not to exceed 60 minutes. Prices start at $60/session.

Private Enrichment and Training

I come to your home during the day and provide enrichment, mental stimulation, basic training, and a bathroom break for your dog for 20 – 45 minutes while you are out. You’ll come home to a calmer, happier dog whose skills are being developed and maintained. I leave you daily reports with the activities we did and the training techniques we practice so that you can implement them as well. Prices start at $20/visit.

Private lessons

Pack walks

Group pack walks are for friendly or mildly reactive dogs. This is a great opportunity for socialization! I will briefly teach clicker training as a positive way to teach leash walking skills with clarity your dog will understand. The price is $10/walk.
