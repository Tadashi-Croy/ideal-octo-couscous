About Us!

Our mission is to provide affordable training and support to every person who wishes to receive it and to help families overcome the anguish and struggles that can sometime happen with our furry friends.

My Dog and Training Buddy:

I own one dog named Lana. She is one of the friendliest and well behaved dogs I have ever had the pleasure of owning, but she didn't start out that way. She was originally abandoned after she was bred out and abandoned once again by her first adoptive family for destructive behavior and aggressiveness towards cats.
Now she has become not only a family member but a valued member of my training team. She has been frequently used by rescues and personal clients in socializing and is an pristine example of what training can accomplish.

My Experiences

I have been working professionally with dogs since 2017 and spend a lot of time volunteering with local animal rescues and shelters. I have fostered dogs in the past, but since having a baby, my focus has been helping foster parents with free one on one training, group classes, learning materials, pack walks, and overnight stays. There is always more to learn from rescue dogs and nothing is more rewarding than seeing them grow into the confident, happy dogs they deserve to be! I graduated from CATCH Canine Trainers Academy in 2020 as a certified dog trainer. I am also a certified Be A Tree child dog bite prevention presenter and a member of the Association of Professional Dog Trainers.

Our Methodology

The science behind dog behavior and training has come a long way over the years and now has proven the effectiveness of positive reinforcement over punishment. Teaching a dog what to do and rewarding them is more effective than simply punishing them for doing something wrong. They need to clearly know what we want them to do, and they need to want to do it. Traditional punishments and corrections increase stress on our dogs and hurts our relationship with them. On the other hand, positive reinforcement methods make learning exciting, good behavior fun, and builds a stronger bond. Training should be enjoyable for everyone involved! I believe in looking at each dog as a whole individual. They all are motivated by different rewards and have different mental and physical needs. By fulfilling all a dog’s needs and building up confidence, they are not only happier and healthier, but ready to listen and learn! It’s easy in our fast-paced lives to miss crucial opportunities to provide much needed enrichment in our dog’s lives. It’s my goal to help you find ways to meet your dog’s needs and implement relevant training that you can realistically fit into your daily life. Consistency is the key to success!
